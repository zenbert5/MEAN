Shades of Light

I see life as spectrum of light in interweaving colors and shades of gray. Life as we would, be a carefree breeze that travels through the light and night or as dancing sparks off smelting bloom. We transcend light that is life.

I am a husband, parent and IT technologist in the Silicon Valley, San Francisco area. For the last two decades I have actively traveled to see the many colors and shades of life.
